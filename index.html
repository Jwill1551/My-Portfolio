About Me

Hello! My name is Joshua Williams and I am a Frontend web dev with a passion for technology and software. I grew up in the city of Inglewood where I as a kid wasn’t exposed to technology. However, once I obtained my first smart phone I was fascinated with applications, which lead me to want to pursue a career in computer science. Currently I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in computer programming. My course work consists of the fundamentals of algorithms, web development, object-oriented design, data structures, and IT disciplines.

This summer, I was selected as one out of fifteen scholars for the inaugural Snap Engineering Academy, where I learned to work with Javascript as well as create applications using backend services like Node.Js with frameworks like APIs and RESTful Routes. During my time there I worked with a group of other scholars to create a Trivia app that relied upon a trivia API to function along with the frontend.

I am fascinated by mobile development, and programming languages that are used to create apps such as kotlin and swift. Along with that I am also interested in graphic design that can support the frontend of web applications. In my free time I like to read and study theology, graphic design, and code on the side for fun.

Feel free to contact me at d.willjoshua@gmail.com.
